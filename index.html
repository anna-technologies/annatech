Our portfolio

QRShield

Nepal's first AI-powered QR code fraud detection platform. QRShield analyses QR patterns in real time, flags suspicious codes before the transaction completes, and integrates directly with payment gateways like ConnectIPS — protecting consumers and businesses from an emerging wave of QR-based financial scams. Actively engaging NRB, Nepal Police Cyber Bureau, and national fintech institutions.

Active Fintech AI/ML Fraud prevention NRB aligned

SoorVibe

Kathmandu's live music discovery platform. SoorVibe connects music lovers with real-time venue listings, artist schedules, and the underground live scene — making Nepal's vibrant music culture discoverable, searchable, and alive. Built for fans, venues, and artists alike, with a warm retro design and a growing network of Kathmandu venues.

Strategic partners

Built on strong alliances

ANNA Technologies amplifies its impact through two foundational strategic partnerships — one in voice AI, one in national research computing.

TX

Telvox — AI Voice Agent Platform

Telvox is ANNA's strategic partner, delivering an enterprise-grade AI voice agent platform built for Nepal's telecom environment. With SIP integrations for NTC and Ncell, Telvox powers intelligent call automation, customer engagement, and business communication at scale — from startups to enterprise — through a structured tier system with transparent pricing and on-demand deployment.

Partner Voice AI NTC · Ncell SIP Enterprise
HPC

HPCNepal — National HPC Initiative

HPCNepal is a not-for-profit national high-performance computing institution anchored by a formal MOU with Kathmandu University. Operating as an ecosystem layer for Nepal's computational future, HPCNepal provides shared supercomputing access to researchers, climate scientists, and institutions — eliminating the compute barrier for Nepal's academic and scientific community. ANNA Technologies is a founding ecosystem partner, providing strategic direction, global outreach, and AI/ML integration support.

Partner HPC Research KU MOU Climate modelling
