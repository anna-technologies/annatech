QRShield

AI-powered QR code fraud detection platform that safeguards Nepal's digital payments ecosystem. QRShield analyses QR patterns in real time, flags suspicious codes before the transaction, and integrates directly with payment gateways like ConnectIPS — protecting consumers and businesses from an emerging wave of QR-based scams.

HPCNepal

A not-for-profit national high-performance computing initiative anchored by an MOU with Kathmandu University. HPCNepal provides shared supercomputing access to researchers, institutions, and climate scientists — eliminating the compute barrier for Nepal's academic and scientific community.
